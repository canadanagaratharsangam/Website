Canada Nagarathar Sangam was established on December 14, 2002 at the Pillayar nonbu function held at Nara Manickam's house in Toronto.

The aims and objectives of CNS are

To organize charitable activities for the underprivileged members of our community like scholarship awards etc.
To help new immigrants settle in Canada.
To exchange ideas and experiences between members.
To organize social activities.
To unite younger nagarathars by imparting our traditional values.
