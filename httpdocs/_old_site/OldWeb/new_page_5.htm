CANADA NAGARATHAR SANGAM (CNS); Toronto Canada.


CNS Cash Scholarships are awarded every year, since 2003, by the Educational Committee of the CNS
to deserving candidates meeting the following eligibility criteria.

Eligibility Criteria:

1. Consistent academic achievements
2. Limited family income
3. Students undertaking professional degree and diploma courses in India.

Please submit the following:

1. Completed application form
2. Letter (not exceeding one page) stating why you should be considered for this scholarship
3. Copy of Mark Certificates
4. Copy of admission letter/ letter from the institution certifying candidate's enrolment.
5. Recent photograph – to be pasted on application form

Cash Scholarships of Indian Rupees 5000 will be awarded to every selected candidate in a year.
Selection in a year, does not guarantee automatic cash scholarship in the subsequent years.
However the selected candidate(s) in a year can also apply for the subsequent years fresh.
Based on the merits, CNS committee will decide the selection of candidates every year.
