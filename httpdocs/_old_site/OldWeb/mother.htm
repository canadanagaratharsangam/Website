MOTHER

Humans are emotional animals. Attachment, social life and empathy are the roots in all of us. This is the Love is Born. Deep emotional attachment between individuals happens in all of our life. The first Love all of us experience is that of our mother. If we can analyze the nature of bondage from conception perhaps we can have a minimal understanding of this relation.

Mother undergoes many hardships during pregnancy. Some of them risk their lives. Yet no woman abandons the role of mother. Even after the hardship of pregnancy and Labour she does go through another one subsequently. Anything you get after hard work gets attached firmly. The relation is here is between two souls. One came out of the blood and tissue of the other.

Even after birth rearing a child is a challenge. I am sure most of us have experienced that in full or in part during our lifetime. Here again Mother sacrifices many things for her so that her child will get the full benefit of this worldly pleasures. They do not eat till the son or daughter comes home even if it is late at night. This sacrifice is inborn to protect the child.

 

If one looks at the other side the child does not have that much fervor towards the mother.

The child takes it for granted that it is the duty of the mother to perform what she is doing. It takes long to realize the nature of Mother’s sacrifice. Once realized the truth attachment becomes tight. Emotions start to play once again between the mother and child.

The word mother itself gives a lot of meaning in various languages. AMMA, MUMMY, MA, and AMME: all of these in various languages mean the same. “IM” means the sound of MagAram in Tamil that is the one from Kundalini or Mooladhara Chakra. Aa means cow in Tamil. Cow means soul again in Tamil. As you can see the word mother in all languages means the delivery of the soul from the root of spine.  Spiritually this is so important. Hence the value for the word mother is immeasurable

Mother is the only soul to understand and appreciate the well being of her children. It is not possible to be born without the Mother and her mammoth sacrifice. It will be a real human gesture to appreciate her role. Such a wonderful day is Mother’s day. It does not end by giving flowers or gifts. Appreciation should come deep from the heart and soul of us. Hence let us pray for the well being of all MOTHERS in the world at this memorable day.

“HAPPY MOTHERS DAY TO ONE AND ALL THE MOTHERS IN THE WORLD”

Chidambaram Yegappan
