STUDENT VOLUNTEER PROGRAM

This is wonderful win-win program for both students and for MACA School.(West End).Student Volunteers, besides getting their hours, they can develop their speaking, facilitation, organization skills while learning or revising their knowledge of Indian Culture and Heritage
.

Under this program, if you are a HighSchool student, you can register with MACA West end school (contact information below) and become a registered Volunteer. The school will engage you for a period of 4 to 6 weeks and provide you a certificate for Volunteer hours. For instance, if you help the MACA West end school for 4 week ends, you can get upto 12 Volunteer hours (i.e. For 4 weeks of 1 hour class and 2 hours preparation time, 4 x 3 =12 hours). Currently we are going to pilot this program and are looking for only 6 Volunteers with a minimum commitment of 4 weeks each
.

Volunteers should be interested in co-facilitating classes on Indian Culture and heritage (study materials will be provided by MACA) to younger students. Volunteers will be supported by the main teachers in the class.High school students with good communication/presentation skills, patience in dealing with younger children and interest in creating innovative methods of teaching using crafts, group activity, painting etc. will be very much preferred.

Interested Parents/Students can contact Jaya Balu at 905-820-2095 or email at jayabalu@yahoo.com. Register yourself as soon as possible since this will be on a first come first served basis. The first class with Volunteers is scheduled to start from January 28 and classes are held every Sunday from 10-12 noon.
